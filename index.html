Solo Leveling

"You have been given a second chance at life.
Will you waste it?"

The Plot

In a world where monstrous creatures emerge from dangerous dungeons, humanity relies on Hunters—individuals with special abilities—to fight for survival. Meet Sung Jin-Woo, a low-ranked Hunter often dubbed the "world's weakest." Struggling to make a name for himself, he faces a life-threatening challenge in a double dungeon, where the odds are stacked against him. However, fate intervenes when he stumbles upon a mysterious power that grants him the ability to level up and grow stronger in ways no Hunter has ever experienced. As Jin-Woo embarks on a journey of transformation, he uncovers hidden secrets, battles fearsome foes, and encounters allies as he strives to rise from the depths of weakness to become the ultimate Hunter. With each step, he faces epic challenges that not only test his strength but also reveal the dark forces lurking behind the dungeons. Solo Leveling is a thrilling tale of redemption, growth, and the relentless pursuit of power that will keep you hooked from start to finish!

The Main Chacreters:

Sung jin-woo

Sung Jin-woo is the main protagonist of "Solo Leveling." Originally a weak E-rank hunter, he was given the opportunity of a lifetime when he was chosen as a Player of the System, granting him the unique ability to grow in strength without limits.

Igris

Igris is one of the first shadows acquired by Sung Jin-woo. Initially, Igris was guarding an empty throne in an instant dungeon where Jin-woo was completing a job change quest.

Beru

Beru is the shadow of the Ant King extracted from Jeju Island. Beru still possesses the ability as a shadow to control an army of shadow ants. Beru is the first shadow of Sung Jin-woo capable of communicating with him and shows a great sense of loyalty. Beru usually addresses Sung Jin-woo as "Sobyeong."

Iron

Abron is an unrestrained cannon who tends to exaggerate in combat, often causing destruction to his surroundings with his actions.

Cha Hae-in

Cha Hae-In is the second vice-master of the Hunters Guild and the Sword Master of the main raiding party. She is the ninth-ranked hunter in South Korea and is also the only female in the S-class.

lee joo-hee

Lee Joo-hee is a former hunter and a temporary partner of Jin 99 during his electronic rank days. She was often seen healing Jin 99 and saved his life several times.
